ABOUT

The longer view

I grew up in India, studied computer science at NIT Bhopal, and joined Amazon in 2018. Eight years later I am still drawn to the same problem: systems that stay up, and the quiet engineering that keeps them that way.

In 2023 I started a master's at the University of Washington's Paul G. Allen School while working full time, finishing in June 2026 with a 3.9 GPA. That stretch pushed my work toward agentic AI: the first production agentic system at my organization, and Ensemble, an independent multi-agent system I have kept in continuous production so I learn the operational failure modes firsthand rather than from a paper.

I was the first in my family to go into this field, so I spend real time mentoring: first-generation CS students at UW through GEN1, hackathon teams at DubHacks and LA Hacks, and young women across Asia through a UNESCO Technovation lab. Seattle is home; the skyline above is the view I picked it for.
