HOUSE RULES

Reliability is the product

Six months of operating a real fleet taught hard lessons. They ship as defaults, not documentation.

DRAFT-FIRST

Nothing leaves without you

Messages to real humans ship as drafts for your approval. Money moves only after an explicit yes. Your agents propose; you dispose.

FRESHNESS

Never stale as fresh

Claims carry timestamps. Old data must declare its age. Health and finance answers re-fetch the source before speaking.

SUPERVISION

Watchdogs all the way down

Health checks, resource alert bands, and supervised restarts keep the fleet alive without you babysitting a terminal.

ONE COORDINATOR

A staff, not a swarm

One coordinator delegates in public and rolls results up to you. Enforced in configuration, so the org chart cannot drift.
